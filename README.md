# Pattle — Pet Battler + Idle RPG

**Play for free in your browser:** https://2fast2hunter-ai.github.io/Pattle/

Pattle is a browser-based pet collection game that blends **turn-based monster battles**, **idle village building**, and a deep **breeding system** — all 100% free, no ads, no pay-to-win.

---

## What is Pattle?

Collect creatures across **25 elemental types**, build your dream team, and battle your way up the arena rankings. While you're offline, your village keeps working — gather resources, hatch eggs, and come back to a stronger roster.

### Core Features

- **Battle** — Turn-based combat with speed-priority, elemental type effectiveness (2× / 0.5×), and critical hits. Multiple modes: Arena farming, Tower (escalating difficulty), Gauntlet (multi-fight survival), and Friend Battles.
- **25 Element Types** — A circular advantage system where each type is strong against 4 and weak against 4. Master the matchups to dominate.
- **Breeding & Genetics** — Pair two pets to produce an egg with inherited traits. Rarity can exceed both parents (1% chance). Predefined fusion recipes and secret hybrid easter eggs reward experimentation.
- **Hatchery** — Real-time countdown timers across multiple egg slots. Watch an ad to speed up hatching.
- **Idle Village** — Resource buildings (mine, lumber, stone quarry) generate Gold, Wood, and Stone while you're away. Upgrade buildings to increase output and storage.
- **Marketplace** — Trade pets and items with other players through a live Firestore-backed marketplace.
- **Daily & Weekly Quests** — Composite rewards (gold + XP + items + badges) that keep progression moving.
- **Lootboxes** — Free daily lootbox (ad-gated) with randomized reveal animations.
- **Social** — Friends list, profile visits, friend battles, and global leaderboards (Tower, Arena, Village).
- **Rarity Tiers** — Common → Uncommon → Rare → Epic → Mythic → Legendary. Higher rarities drop more rarely and have better base stats.
